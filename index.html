Analytics & Machine Learning

Flight Delay Prediction

To tackle the flight delay prediction problem, I started by cleaning and preparing the dataset to make it ready for analysis. Then, I explored the data to understand the main factors contributing to delays—things like time of day, airline, and weather patterns. Using those insights, I built two machine learning models with Random Forest: one to classify whether a flight would be delayed by more than 15 minutes, and another to predict exactly how many minutes it might be delayed.

Consultancy

Underperforming Food Courts

The approach focuses on improving low conversion rates in Tier-2 city food courts by identifying key issues through surveys—such as high prices, poor hygiene, slow service, and lack of local food appeal. It analyzes the customer journey from mall entry to repeat visits, revealing major drop-offs. Based on consumer behavior (especially Gen Z, millennials, and families), the strategy recommends targeted fixes like QR self-order kiosks, local food stalls, visible hygiene audits, and loyalty programs. A 6-month pilot in two cities is proposed, aiming for a 15% conversion increase and ₹2.5 Cr in added revenue, with ROI expected in under 6 months.

Consultancy

Industry Analysis: E-Commerce

This project presents a comprehensive analysis of the e-commerce industry, with a particular focus on the Indian and global digital retail landscape. The study explores how technology, consumer behavior, market segmentation, and competitive dynamics shape the evolution of online commerce. Through a combination of market data, business model evaluation, value chain analysis, and user personas, the report examines key players such as Amazon, Flipkart, Nykaa, Myntra, and Meesho, uncovering their strengths, innovations, and strategic differences.
